Assignment #3

This is a form I built using some JavaScript and the Mandrill RESTful API. It allows me to accept emails from people who visit my webpage without giving out my email address for spammers / what not to get a hold of it. Mandrill lets me make a POST request to their API, and using some JSON I can send an email in JavaScript, as Mandril actually sends the email, and I don't need to deal with having my own email server. Mandril is also free up to like 2,000 emails so its worth playing with for such a simple site.
